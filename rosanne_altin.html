About Me

With a passion for service in her Los Angeles community, Rosanne is proud to represent “Teacher Next Door” as a Preferred Agent. She believes that every day is a new opportunity to make a difference in our communities. Assisting fellow educators in finding their dream home is a top priority for Rosanne. She understands the needs of teachers and the Southern California real estate market, working with clients to make the buying or selling process enjoyable and seamless.

Prior to her Real Estate career, she taught many years in the Los Angeles public schools as a Health Educator and Athletic Coach. Originally from St. Louis, Missouri, and now an Angelino for the past 30 years, Rosanne attended Washburn University and received a Bachelor of Education degree in Health Sciences. She continued her post-graduate education at California State University Northridge, studying Adaptive Kinesiology.

Teaching and coaching helped develop effective interpersonal skills that are invaluable to her clients. Rosanne’s gift of enthusiasm and positive energy coupled with her excellent communication and collaboration skills ideally suits her as a partner you can rely on to help you navigate the real estate market's complexities.

Rosanne holds high the values of hard work and integrity, consistently providing her clients with personalized and caring service. Her clients can expect and will receive the respect and loyalty they deserve in their Real Estate journey. Together your homeownership goals will become a reality. “My Passion is Your New Home.” Is her motto.

In her spare time, Rosanne is an avid hiker and backpacker, traveling California National and State Park trails, practices yoga, weight trains, and loves cooking and old fashion road trips.
