About Me

Rosalind Leonard is a mother of 4 daughters and grandmother, or nana as her 7 grandchildren call her. The eldest of 3 girls, she was an only child until the age of 10. Her pre-school years were often spent dancing in the window of one of the cleaners and record shops owned by her parents, which is no doubt where Rosalind got her outgoing personality and entrepreneurial spirit.

Becoming a Real Estate professional has always been a dream. Rosalind was first licensed in November of 1998. She specialized in market analysis, honed her skills with some of the best in the industry and became a uniquely qualified top producing professional. After a family leave of absence, Roz returned to the industry as a Preferred Agent Partner ready and excited to assist clients in reaching their home buying goals.
