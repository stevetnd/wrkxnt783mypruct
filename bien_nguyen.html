About Me

Hey there! My name is Bien Nguyen (pronounced “B.N. Win”). Welcome to my site! I have a diverse background in business, construction, and local government, which I'm now transferring to real estate. As someone who has worked closely with public safety, and with several teachers in my family, I am excited to be a part of the Teacher Next Door program and serve my community!

I originally chose to go into real estate because I needed a way to provide for my family, flexibility to be around for my boys, while also giving me a way to help people. My prior background included being a certified facilitator/trainer - and what better way to make a difference for others than by guiding them in the biggest purchase of their lives - their home!

The best praise I ever received was from my sales manager. who said that he stopped getting calls as soon as I took over. My strong project management skills and responsiveness to others took the load off of his shoulders. Helping people and providing exceptional customer service drives everything that I do!

When I am not helping people, I can be found baking yummy desserts (cheesecakes, cakes, pies, and more), and making music with my husband.

I look forward to working with you!
