About Me

Whether you are buying or selling, you can rest assured that I’ll devote every effort to providing you with representation that is tailored to your unique situation. By presenting you with a fluid layout of the home buying or selling process, complete with visual images, that are easy and straight-forward to understand, I can eliminate the confusion and stress that can oftentimes, accompany the home buying or selling process.

As the daughter of one of the largest home builders in the Palm Springs, Yucca Valley California areas, I am passionate about real estate! I’ve been involved in every aspect of home building, from marketing and development to value and design, all of which give me a distinct edge as a broadly-skilled residential sales agent.  My passion, coupled with the desire to make your home buying or selling experience seamless and pleasant, strikes the perfect balance between exuberance and the essential understanding of today’s real estate market! An educator for more than two decades, the wife to a retired military veteran, and mother to three amazing children, I’ve had the opportunity to experience, in-depth, various neighborhoods throughout the country. Aesthetically, professionally, along with educational and community resources, home valuations and entertainment, Las Vegas has truly proven to be one of the most versatile, opportunity rich communities in the country. From the breath-taking mountains that surround the valley, to the stunning sunsets that incapsulate the Las Vegas strip skyline, there’s something in this city for everyone! Whether you’re looking for a high-tech home where Alexa is your dutiful personal assistant, a swanky, sophisticated high-rise with views of the strip, or a cozy, quaint and charming hidden community surrounded by excellent schools and parks, the Las Vegas valley has it ready and waiting for you!

As for my educational background, I hold a Bachelor of Arts degree in Psychology from Loyola Marymount University, a Master of Arts Degree in Education from University of Phoenix and Educational Administration from Sierra Nevada College. Overall, the cohesive bond that encapsulates my life experiences, education, and family have helped to mold me into a person who is passionate, fiercely loyal, strategic, and authentic! Anyone who has ever worked with me will attest to the fact that I am honest and down to earth! I pride myself on my keen ability to multi-task and to ensure that my clients know unequivocally, that they are my number one priority!
