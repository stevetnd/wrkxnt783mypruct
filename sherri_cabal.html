About Me

I was born and raised in the New Orleans area. I come from a background of 20+ years in the Judicial System as well as 10+ years in the Jefferson Parish Public School System and I bring knowledge of my customer service experience to bear when working with my real estate clients.

I attended the University of New Orleans and earned a Bachelor's degree in Elementary Education, my skills in dealing with a diverse population gives me a unique insight into how to communicate and bargain for my clients. I entered the Real Estate world in order to help provide better for my family and have not looked back.

I have been married for 29 years and live in River Ridge, Louisiana with my husband Steve and son Conner. I spend my free time with my family and friends making memories that will last a lifetime.
