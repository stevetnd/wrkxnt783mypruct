About Me

Raised in a 'real estate' family in Southern California, Jessica is a 3rd generation real estate professional that epitomizes integrity, energy, determination, and creativity. After college, Jessica began her professional career as a Wellness Coordinator for a premier senior living company. During her tenure here she identified her passion for seniors and their stories they shared. Jessica was relocated to Northern California to open a community in 2003. Jessica opened 3 assisted living facilities and worked in nearly every capacity when she realized her love of sales and serving. Jessica continued her career in sales and marketing, specializing in helping medium to large companies with brand continuity.

After traveling and living throughout the Western US, Jessica and her family returned home to Southern California in 2018 to raise their kids and be closer to her aging grandparents. Soon after, Jessica followed her passion and teamed up with the highly esteemed and coveted expert of probate real estate transactions in Los Angeles, otherwise known as her mentor and Father, Jerry Redmond. Jessica specializes in working with Seniors, Trusts, and Probate sales. She has over 20 years of experience working directly with Seniors, their families, and trusted advisors. Jessica has applied the same determination, patience, and passion her grandmother did as a Realtor in the '70s, '80s, and '90s.

To keep abreast of the ever-changing industry, Jessica aligns herself with some of the top agents in the US and California specializing in working with Seniors and Probate. Jessica is truly committed to always raise the bar, maintain a high level of professionalism, and make a one-of-a-kind impression on each client that she has the pleasure of working with.

A true expert in her area, Jessica lives with her husband Justin, retired US Navy, and their 4 kids in Corona. Jessica loves to serve her community, veterans like her husband, and plays as much as possible!
