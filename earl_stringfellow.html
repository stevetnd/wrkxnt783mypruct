About Me

Earl Stringfellow graduated from the University of Phoenix with a Bachelor Degree in business management. Earl began his career in the financial industry in 1998 as a bank teller for Home Savings of America Bank in California. It was through a lucrative career in banking and lending that Earl discovered his passion for providing personalized solutions for people, and a love for helping others achieve the dream of home ownership. In the four years that Earl has been a Realtor, he has been able to utilize this knowledge of banking and lending to become a trusted advisor to his clients while assisting them in transacting on their dream homes.

Earl is native to San Diego California, where he attended and graduated from Kearny High School. Earl completed 3 years at San Diego State University before transferring to The University of Phoenix due to his dedication to advancing in the financial industry. He worked in many areas of retail banking and lending before being transferred to Arizona. Earl and his Wife Janell relocated to Phoenix Arizona in 2003 where he continued to build his network and contacts in the financial industry. It is the many relationships that he has built over the years that allows him to be a valuable center of influence who continues to provide unlimited resources for his clients.
