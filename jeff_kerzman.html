About Me

I became a Realtor® to provide the great art of customer service. I’m instilled with a passion to ensure that you, my client, are the priority and at the forefront of everything. Client relationships are never “one size fits all” and adapting to each client’s individual needs and expectations is of utmost importance to me.

You’ll find that I’m outgoing, personable and fun to work with every step of the way. I’m also detail-oriented, organized and will strive to gain your absolute trust throughout the transaction by always going the extra mile for you. Communication is vital, and I’ll be there to answer all your questions and keep you in the loop.

What I thoroughly enjoy about being an agent is helping people from all walks of life. Meeting new people is always a fun adventure. I enjoy the challenge of meeting client needs, whether negotiating the best deal on the behalf of a seller or finding that dream home that ticks all the boxes for a buyer. I consider myself a partner, not a business or sales associate, when it comes to finding the best home for your family. And I will work tirelessly to represent you, advocate for you and exceed all your expectations.

Outside of real estate, I enjoy traveling and exploring new destinations which reflects my many years in the travel industry. I am a natural when it comes to hosting dinner parties and I’m an avid event planner for all things fun! I currently reside in the Platt Park/Washington Park area, but I’m familiar with all of Denver’s central neighborhoods—Capitol Hill, Congress Park and City Park to name a few. I really enjoy areas with walkability where you have easy access to coffee shops, restaurants and local businesses.

I look forward to assisting with your real estate journey. Feel free to get in touch!
