About Me

Lisa Rayman’s passion for construction and real estate started from the ground up – literally. Growing up in Toronto, she used to join her father – a contractor – on job sites, and even helped build the family cabin.

It’s that influence that kick-started her love of design and building, while also fueling her entrepreneurial spirit. After obtaining her Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology with and option in Nutrition, Lisa started a Chiropractic office and other Non-profit companies. But design and real estate was in her blood, and after relocating to Sacramento, she rediscovered her passion for real estate.

With living, playing, working, and a 10 year old son very active in sports, Lisa is very familiar with areas of Arden Park, Sierra Oaks, Carmichael, River Park, East Sacramento, Curtis Park, Land Park, Tahoe Park and Natomas. Whether you’re relocating to the area, purchasing your first home, selling your fifth investment property, or building a custom home from the ground up, Lisa’s there to help her clients every step of the way. With her integrity, professionalism, and attention detail, Lisa is committed to help her clients achieve their dreams, whatever they may be.

Lisa is excited to be part of the prestigious Coldwell Banker Residential Brokerage brand, one of the most widely respected and trusted real estate names in the nation.

Here are some of the things Lisa can do for you:

Sell a Home: When it's time to move, you need someone who will advertise your home, show to prospective buyers, negotiate the purchase contract, arrange financing, oversee the inspections, handle all necessary paperwork and supervise the closing. I can take care of everything you need, from start to close.

Consult on Home Selling Tactics: Oftentimes buyers don't visualize living in your home the way you do. I can make your home attractive to its ideal audience - which can help you get top dollar.
