About Me

Melissa Mims and The Mims Home Team specialize in residential property in Houston and surrounding areas. Interested in buying, selling or leasing? Look no further, The Mims Home Team will seamlessly guide you through the process of selling your home quickly or finding that perfect home.

Solid, financial decisions are the focus. Melissa understands the complexities of investing in real estate and provides expert advise. Completing exhaustive analysis of properties/opportunities is where Melissa sets herself apart from other agents. Whether working with a savvy investor or a 1st-time home-buyer, Melissa does not miss a beat.

Melissa is a Licensed Real Estate Broker, has over a decade of experience and extensive experience noted by her numerous, professional designations: Graduate Realtor Institute, Short Sales and Foreclosure Certification, Senior Real Estate Specialist, Certified Negotiation Expert, Accredited Home-Staging Specialist and Certified Social Media Pro. Melissa is also a graduate of the prestigious 2017 Texas Realtor Leadership Program.

Melissa takes time to listen to clients so she can formulate an action plan to cater to their specific needs. Whether selling quickly at a premium or finding the perfect property for her buyers and lease clients, Melissa works to exceed expectations by utilizing every tool possible to find success.

Melissa has immersed herself in the various aspects of real estate. She has personally flipped multiple properties, purchased foreclosures, owns income property (rentals) and has successfully guided clients through the short-sale process. This allows her to speak from experience, to benefit her clients. Call her 24/7 when you are ready to work with a full-time real estate expert that will exceed all your real estate needs. She is a member of NAR, TAR and HAR.

Although Melissa works primarily in and around "The Loop" in Houston, The Mims Home Team assists buyers and sellers throughout Houston and surrounding areas. They have clients from Conroe to League City, from Sugar Land to Channelview, from Cypress to Pasadena. Essentially, they can assist buyers and sellers anywhere in and around Harris County.
