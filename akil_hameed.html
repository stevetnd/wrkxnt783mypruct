About Me

As Founder and Chief Executive Officer of FASS Real Estate Services (FASS), Akil Hameed has progressively grown the company which currently manages more than $80,000,000 in real estate, consisting of 350+ scattered site residential and commercial properties in Northeast Ohio and its surrounding suburbs.

At FASS, Hameed directs a team of over 20+ Brokers to develop and implement marketing strategies that expand awareness and recognition of the company and its many products and services into the community. As an experienced property manager and business professional, Hameed has an established record of success with top-level project management, property management, and operation functions. Hameed began providing Receivership Property Management Services in 2015 to the banking institutions; working with CBRE on a Joint venture project. Today, FASS Real Estate Services has over 250,000 sq ft of assets under current receivership orders.

Hameed brings more than ten years of experience in the real estate industry including acquisition and analysis, development, finance, leasing, management, marketing, receivership and operations; and has a proven ability to analyze business processes, visualize improvements, and orchestrate changes that impact growth and development.
