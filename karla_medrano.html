About Me

I am very HONEST; I keep everything real and upfront with my clients. Not everything is going to be easy or pretty, so it is important to me to bring light to the negatives that a buyer or seller may not see. I ensure that my clients and I are constantly on the same page, their wellbeing is my main priority.

I treat my clients the way I wish to be treated. I remember purchasing my first home and needing guidance throughout the whole process, but did not receive it. I now wish to provide that guidance to you. I am aggressive and experienced when it comes to negotiating for my clients; I will give 110% in my fight to provide the best for you!

I educate my clients. Homework is a must for potential buyers and sellers. I make sure they read my buyers & sellers guides, write down any questions they may have, and quiz them to ensure we are on the same page. These will help my clients understand the terminology that is frequently used throughout the buying/selling process. I will also ensure that my clients understand the time line of everything that will happen during the transaction. This is to ensure there are no unpleasant surprises along the way.

I have technology on my side. Technology and social media have become an essential part of the real estate industry. I have invested my own time and money to remain educated on the cutting edge of technology that can be utilized in selling and buying a home. This ensures that my clients are receiving the best and most up to date customer service I can offer.
