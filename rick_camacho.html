About Me

Rick Camacho is a California native who understands the pulse of the real estate market in the bay area. With 11 years of experience and 14 years of serving in the United States Army as a former Drill Sergeant, Rick leads the way in helping his clients make the best decisions in purchasing their new home!

Graduating with a Bachelor of Arts in Communications from California State University of Sacramento and a Master of Business Administration, closing deals is his passion.

Whether you want to live in the city or the beautiful suburbs the right location is waiting for you. Listings with easy public transportation, parks, recreation and easy to access nearby dinners on the town, you will feel as if you made the best financial decision of a lifetime.

Buying or selling your home can be a complex process that requires a resourceful professional and confidence that they are working on your behalf.

Rick Camacho will work along with you to connect you to the right lenders and if you have one, he will be there willing and ready to work with them as a team. Knowledgeable in special loan programs for Veterans, FHA, Conventional and Teachers next door programs you are sure to have a quick plan of action to purchase your new home or sell your home with the best expertise around.
