About Me

Chrystal Ueltschi grew up in the Hampton Roads area. She earned a Master of Education from Old Dominion University and currently resides in Virginia Beach. After having her 3 children and establishing a teaching career, she decided she wanted to help people find their first home, dream home, or vacation beach house. Being a former teacher, she understands the financial constraints that can affect educators in obtaining their very own home. She has always had a vision of being a realtor and assisting people with buying and selling. Her first home purchase was right here in Virginia Beach. Her realtor's help and assistance with the purchase reminded her of how she had always wanted to begin a career in real estate and experience the same joy on other's faces as she had when she acquired her first set of keys from a closing. She is also excited to assist sellers sell their homes and help them experience their next adventure in life.

Accomplishments and Affiliations: Honestly I am a new agent so I don't have much. I do have an excellent eye for detail and I am willing to work for my buyers and sellers. When I purchased my first home, I did a ton of research and taught my realtor a few things that she did not know. I am extremely quick with my math and very optimistic about the potential in homes. Not sure if it helps but I watch a LOT of HGTV and love imagining the potential in all properties.
