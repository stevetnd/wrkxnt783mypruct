About Me

Get to know your next REALTOR®…that would be me, Linda Watts. I am thrilled to assist with your next home sale or purchase in this great Arizona real estate market! I am excited to service your home buying and home selling needs in both Maricopa and Pinal counties, and my main areas of focus are Chandler, Gilbert, Queen Creek, Mesa, Scottsdale, Phoenix, and Maricopa.

As a licensed professional in Arizona, I work hard to provide the trust, communication, and dedication that you deserve so that all your real estate needs and goals are accomplished. While motivated to assist First-Time Home Buyers, Veterans and Active Duty Military, I welcome the opportunity to work with all sellers and buyers. I possess over 25 years of experience in accounting and business, which attribute to the attention to detail when writing and negotiating contracts and ensuring a smooth and successful closing. I am a member of the National Association of REALTORS®, Arizona Association of REALTORS®, Arizona Regional Multiple Listing Service, and various community networking groups. My company, EXIT Realty Expanded Vision, is an A+ rated company with the Better Business Bureau, has memberships with the Gilbert Chamber of Commerce, United States Association of Accredited Businesses, and the National Association of Professional Women. I recently earned my certifications as an Accredited Buyer Representative (ABR), Seller Representative Specialist (SRS) and Military Relocation Professional (MRP).

What makes Linda Watts the best REALTOR® for your home buying and home selling needs? I have years of experience as an Interior Designer and Professional Stager of Linda Watts Interior Design, which allows me to sell your home faster and for more money by offering staging and home preparation recommendations that will attract qualified buyers. I add a touch of class to help you decorate your new home so that you have a place that you will absolutely love and enjoy every day!

About me, personally…I am originally from California, but have roots in Seattle, WA. We made Arizona our new home because of the wonderful career opportunities, the sunny weather, and not to mention the amazing real estate market. I am very much a family-oriented person and my personality is outgoing and upbeat. I believe in living life to the fullest and I attract people who are genuine and positive, and with whom I can build long-term relationships.
