About Me

My name is Barbara Brockus and I specialize in helping buyers and sellers purchase and sell real estate nationwide. In the Phoenix Metropolitan area, I have concentrated in the east and west valley cities of beautiful Ahwatukee, Phoenix, Tempe, Chandler, Mesa, Gilbert, Sun Lakes, and Scottsdale. Being able to wake up every morning and see the scenic mountain views all around you with bike paths, hiking and walking trails, golf courses, and many recreational activities all around…this is what makes Arizona one of the most desirable places to live. If you can picture this type of peace and serenity in your life, in a place where the sun shines an average of 333 days per year, contact me today so we can find the perfect home for you.

I have been In the real estate business for over 11 years and have a background and experience in marketing, sales, and education. I hold a Master of Business Administration (MBA), a Bachelor’s Degree in Marketing from Arizona State University (go Sun Devils!), and a Secondary Education Teaching credential from Ottawa University. My passion for learning and continuous education has also led me to obtain multiple real estate designations. I am excited about real estate, and I serve as an effective resource to my clients as I help successfully navigate them through the selling and buying processes.

Outside of real estate, I grew up in the “show-me” state of Missouri. After graduating from high school, I relocated to Arizona, graduated from college, and later married my wonderful husband. We have a terrific son and have resided in the Ahwatukee Foothills for over 15 years. In my spare time, I enjoy weightlifting, hiking, and spending time with family and friends.

Through the years of professional growth and development, I discovered that I enjoy helping people. Becoming a REALTOR® was the best decision of my career. During my tenure, I have found that, in order to be successful, you have to be genuine in your dealings with people and be willing to put their needs and best interests above your own throughout the entire process. It’s all about having a positive impact on someone else’s life.

If you want a true professional to help you take the guesswork out of the buying and selling process, I’m your girl. Call me, Barbara Brockus, at 602-421-2651. Download my mobile business card by texting BARBB to 85377.
