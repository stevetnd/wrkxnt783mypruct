About Me

Real estate isn't just a job to Maria Kharot, it's a way of life! She started working as a receptionist in a real estate office at 14 and her career took of from there. Today she is an experienced and well-respected Coldwell Banker agent serving home buyers and sellers in Chicago's north and northwest suburbs as well as globally.

"I know every aspect of the real estate transaction, said Maria Kharot." I provide the additional services of helping my clients understand what they are purchasing or selling. I have worked as a real estate paralegal for over four years, a property manager for over 10 years and a real estate agent for over six years," she said. "I review condo documents, title commitments, understand municipality requirements. I run the whole nine yards and make sure my clients understand everything."

Whether someone is buying or selling, real estate is an emotional business transaction. Maria's clients know she will be honest and serve them with the highest integrity. Additionally, Maria leverages Coldwell Banker's innovative tools and resources to ensure her clients have the most updated market knowledge and access to the company's unique marketing programs.

Maria is a life long resident of the City of Chicago and grew up in the Wrigleyville neighborhood. She currently lives in the northern suburbs.

An avid shopper, Maria Kharot is a strong believer in retail therapy. She also enjoys sports, traveling, art and spending time with her family which includes four children. Call Maria when you are looking for an agent who can do it all!
