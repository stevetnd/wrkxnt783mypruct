About Me

Eze Amadi has been in the financial services industry for over 13 years. Within the real estate industry, Eze has worked with individuals from various backgrounds, ethnicities, and economic levels. He connects on a personal level with the client to understand their specific needs and provide coaching throughout the process. Eze enjoys working with first time home buyers to reach the homeownership milestone and start their wealth journey. Wherever you may be in the home buying process, Eze will connect with you to explain and guide you from beginning to end. Eze is passionate about finding dream homes and facilitating ownership especially for minority populations. For Eze, it’s not just about selling a house, but also making sure that the buyer is prepared and financially able to maintain the property that they desire.

As a Prince Georges County native and Morgan State University graduate in Baltimore, Maryland, Eze has vast knowledge of the DMV area. He has a great understanding of the neighborhoods and future planning for the area. On a personal note, Eze has a lovely wife and two children, a 3 year old son and an infant daughter. In his free time Eze teaches financial literacy and promotes childhood literacy through his non-profit organization. He also is a huge sports fan. Contact Eze today, his motto is “Adding value to your real estate transaction”.
