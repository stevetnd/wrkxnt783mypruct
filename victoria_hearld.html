About Me

Victoria Hearld, Broker/Owner, Victoria Hearld & Associates LLC

Originally from Monroe LA, I relocated to the Dallas metroplex in 1999 after graduating from Northeast Louisiana University with a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration, with a focus in Computer Information Systems. After an enjoyable experience working at Microsoft, I switched gears and moved into the Sales and Mortgage arenas. In 2010, I settled into my dream career as a real estate agent with Ebby Halliday Realtors, where I gained invaluable training and experience as a Realtor.

As broker and owner of Victoria Hearld & Associates LLC, a real estate business built on honesty, integrity and perseverance, two of my greatest strengths are treating my clients how I’d like to be treated and my sincere passion for helping others sell their real estate and find just the right property to call home. I am confident that my past clients will agree that I remain open to learning new ideas, that I constantly think outside of the box for solutions, and if I don’t have an answer at the moment, I will do whatever it takes to find it. Maintaining my composure under pressure and developing a positive relationship with all parties involved have been key attributes in successfully working through real estate transactions.

I am well aware that there are so many real estate brokerages to consider, so I am especially grateful to all of my past, present and future clients, who trust me to assist with one of the largest financial transactions that they will ever make. Whenever you, your family or friends have any real estate needs, I would be honored to be your real estate agent of choice!
