About Me

My name is Dennis Amundson and I am a Realtor with eXp Realty. I started out as a Mortgage Lender for over 10 years before becoming a Realtor.

My goals and aspirations are to continue helping families achieve their dreams and goals with their Real Estate needs. It brings me much happiness seeing the amazing and emotional feelings that come from someone buying or selling a home! Knowing mortgage and real estate helps me understand and anticipate certain things that come up through a real estate sale. Having this knowledge helps my clients to be ready for almost anything!

I have been very blessed to have had so many people entrust me with the biggest financial purchases of their lives. Because of their trust I have had some amazing achievements and accomplishments.
