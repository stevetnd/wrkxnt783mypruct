About Me

With full time licensed experience since the year 2000, Jim currently owns and manages Green Tree Realty Solutions, Inc. for the benefit of his many residential real estate clients. Often considered an “innovator”, Jim thrives on creating solutions. For years Jim has worked within the real estate industry to solve marketing and mortgage financing challenges for his clients.

After formative years in technical sales, Jim’s various real estate accomplishments include unique client service programs such as: 3D Virtual Showcase Tours Online, a Guaranteed Seller Satisfaction Marketing Commitment, the Residential Lease-back Sale, a Newly Built Home Buyer’s Allowance, the “Make It Your Own” program with professional assistance and many various Buyer’s Agency services.

For every challenging Home Buyer and Seller situation, Jim believes there is at least one or more win-win solution. No matter a home's location, feature, condition, or the client's financial challenges, Green Tree Realty Solutions, Inc. offers the best solutions available, at the best available price.
