About Me

nventive. streetwise. effervescent.

With a longstanding background in sales, Deanna Locke has continually achieved in her roles due to a high-achieving attitude and personable demeanor. While working for ADP, it was her clients—comprised of accountants and realtors— who encouraged Deanna to shift into, and ultimately excel, in real estate where she enjoys more significant personal connections. Now, Deanna fulfills numerous roles as a high-producing broker—communicator, negotiator, designer—all with a flair of fun.

Prior to receiving her license, Deanna branched into a marketing director role and assisted with launching a successful local company. It’s through this position that Deanna developed creative marketing strategies and solutions, which she now applies to real estate.

Her connections, jobs and community have provided Deanna with a high volume of transactions, and in turn, has jumpstarted her vast experience and expertise in Denver’s resale market. When conflicts arise, she’s proactive in providing and communicating resolutions to her clients. Her goal is to guide clients seamlessly throughout the process, catering to a positive experience every step of the way.
