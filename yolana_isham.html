About Me

My name is Yolana Isham and I am super excited to help buyers and sellers who want to purchase or sell a home here in Arizona’s Valley of the Sun! While I enjoy working with all types of buyers and sellers, I especially love assisting and serving First-Time Buyers, Active-Duty Military/Veterans, Teachers/School Staff, First Responders, and Doctors/Nurses/Medical Professionals. I am happy to be part of the amazing Teacher Next Door program as it is an opportunity for me to show my appreciation and give back to individuals and families who have made a huge contribution to our country, our children, our health, and our overall well-being!

I have been a licensed REALTOR® in the state of Arizona since 2006. My specializations are in home purchases, home sales, military relocation, senior living, investment properties, and second homes, and my focus areas include Gilbert, Chandler, Mesa, Queen Creek, San Tan Valley, Tempe, Phoenix, Ahwatukee, Laveen, Maricopa, Scottsdale, and the surrounding areas. I am the proud Broker/Owner of EXIT Realty Expanded Vision, located in Gilbert, AZ, one of the most desired cities in the country and my company maintains an A+ rating with the Arizona Better Business Bureau (BBB).

I count it a privilege and an honor be part of the one of the biggest investments you will ever make...the place you find safety and security...the place you create many happy memories...the place you may raise your children (or pets)...the place you allow your personality to shine through your own personal touch...THE PLACE YOU CALL HOME! I view real estate as an opportunity to use my knowledge and expertise to help you expand your vision so, together, we can implement a plan to accomplish your goals of home ownership. I focus on building lasting relationships which is why 95% of my business comes from referrals and wonderful past and present repeat clients.
