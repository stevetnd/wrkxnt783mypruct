About Me

"Wyoming and Cheyenne Equal HOME!" My name is Pam Pafford and I have called Cheyenne and Wyoming home for 30yrs so to me this is "HOME"

I started my journey of becoming a Realtor in 2017 and have loved every minute of it since. I specialize in city and rural residential, new home construction, military relocation, and first-time homebuyers. I am always eager to learn and further my education within my career so I can better serve my clients. Cheyenne is a great community and I have been lucky enough to help and serve our great community in several different ways from volunteering with different organizations and clubs to serving on the Cheyenne Board of Realtors as the Public Relations Committee Chair for 2021.

Whether it is building your dream home, selling your first home to grow with your family or purchasing your first home, I look forward to helping you every step of the way. My service to you does not stop when you receive the keys to your new home or when you shut the door on your current home for the last time. I look forward to helping you through this next stage in your life.
