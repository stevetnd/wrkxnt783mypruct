About Me

Cheri is a native of Chicago and moved to Plant City, near Tampa Bay, Florida in 1984. She began her career with Teacher Next Door in 2011, first as a Realtor and Sales Associate, then as a Broker Associate. In 2018 Cheri became Teacher Next Door's Principal Broker.

Cheri has dedicated her career to helping others and is excited to give back to the teachers, nurses, law enforcement, firefighters and others who do so much in our local communities. She posses a unique understanding of their housing needs and dedicates herself to being available to them whenever they need her. Powerful communication skills, organization and negotiation skills, along with her friendly, personable demeanor is what Cheri brings to every client relationship.
