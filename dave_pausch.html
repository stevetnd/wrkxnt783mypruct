About Me

David Pausch began his real estate practice in 2017 with the intention of helping first time homebuyers achieve their dreams of home ownership. He is a longtime Madisonian who currently resides in the Rolling Meadows neighborhood. He is deeply attached to the city and is committed to helping to ensure that all Madisonians, regardless of background, can become homeowners.

When not helping Madison’s diverse communities achieve their real estate goals, he is a working professional theatre artist who is one of the founders of Guest House Theatre, Madison’s newest professional theatre company. He is a committed conservationist who actively educates his clients on the importance of making high efficiency home choices (including solar energy, air sealing and insulating, and the addition of native plants to their home landscapes) to improve their home’s value and reduce their carbon footprint. He is a two-time graduate of the University of Wisconsin-Madison and holds the GREEN certification from the National Association of Realtors and is a Real Estate Ambassador for Focus on Energy.
