About Me

Danielle is a Realtor with eXp Realty in Dallas, Texas. Danielle brings her social media marketing experience to the Real Estate world & helps to educate & empower the next generation of homebuyers.

As your Realtor, Danielle will be an advocate for you. Whether you are buying, selling or renting, she will work tirelessly to promote your best interested. If you are buying, Danielle will work hard to provide you with the most up-to-date market data to inform & assist you in writing an offer so you can have the strongest negotiating position. If you are selling, she will work with you to get the best price for your home, in the lease amount of time, by combining her knowledge of social media marketing & eXp Realty's superior marketing materials.

Danielle understands that buying & selling real estate can be stressful. As a full-time Realtor, she will work hard to lower your stress level & make your experience as pleasant & easy as possible. She will accomplish this through constant communication & by being accessible whenever you need her.

Danielle is a multi-faceted entrepreneur. In addition to selling real estate, Danielle runs a successful network marketing business. She has been a Top Income Earner & Top Leader in the company & runs a massive organization.

When not assisting clients or leading her team, Danielle enjoys creating social media content across all platforms; including Instagram, TikTok & Facebook. She also enjoys spending time with her boyfriend, Dustin, their 2 dogs & kitten: Lucy, Sadie & Kitty. Additionally, they love traveling the world, spending time with family & eating delicious food!
