About Me

Trisha Cole, Associate Broker for North & Co. Real Estate is a 16-+years residential real estate veteran and has built a solid reputation as tireless advocate for her clients.

She initially obtained her license in 2003 and found immediate success when she sold her first home in only two weeks. This early success was a sign of things to come when as she gained experience, she began to receive awards for her work. She has won such prestigious awards as the multi-million-dollar producer award twice and brokerage top producer awards many times. She decided to obtain her broker’s license in 2008 and has had several roles as a Designated Broker. In this capacity she has had a great opportunity to work with many homeowners in various markets. This has allowed her to be well rounded in all areas of Real Estate. From short sales, REO, residential resale and new homes sales. The key to her business is education. She was formally a certified trainer in Real Estate educating agents and her many clients of our forever changing Real Estate climate.

Despite her larger successes Trisha’s takes a personal approach to helping her clients navigate challenges with as little stress as possible. She epitomizes integrity, energy, hard work and creative service. Trisha is a family woman devoted to God, her husband, who is a Veteran and 4 children. If there are four words you can use to describe Trisha, integrity, committed, diligent and tenacious.

She recently decided to partner with Teacher Next Door hoping to narrow her focus on the clients she enjoys working with the most, educators, Veterans, and first responders.
