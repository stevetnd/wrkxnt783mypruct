About Me

The Maple Knoll Realtors Team consists of Christina Cifalino, Frank Cifalino and Sandy Campione. Together they have a combined real estate experience of almost 20 years. Christina and Frank have been active community members in Wayne for over twenty years, raising 4 children who were enrolled in both the public and private school systems. Christina is currently an active professional in Wayne, working as a teacher in the Wayne Public School System for more than 15 years. With the support of her team members, they are all well-versed in the abundance of resources that Wayne has to offer its residents and the reasons Wayne Schools are consistently ranked among the top 75 in the state!

As a special education teacher, Christina naturally supports and advocates for others. Her experience and ability to listen and understand the needs of others allows her to form trustworthy relationships where her leadership is valued.

Frank has been an active sales associate for the duration of his career, spanning the last 30 years. He has dedicated himself to understanding what his clients are looking for and actively searches around the clock to fulfill their needs. Frank’s energy and his natural desire to please his clients makes him a remarkable agent.

Sandy’s expertise and knowledge in real estate is second to none. She has the natural ability to understand her client’s needs and advocate for them from the beginning to the end of each and every transaction. She’s on top of her game and oversees that the process goes as smoothly as possible.

The Maple Knoll Realtors Team specializes in the support and advocacy for those seeking to purchase or sell their home given their individual circumstances.
