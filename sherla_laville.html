About Me

Sherla Alexander-Laville’s knowledge and experience in real estate provides her with a unique view of the real estate market that she shares with her clients to help them make the best decisions for their needs and desires. Based in Montpelier, Sherla is the expert that will help you with your home buying and selling needs in Prince Georges, Anne Arundel, Montgomery and Baltimore Counties. Specializing in first-time home buyers and listing homes for sale, Sherla has the expertise necessary to assist her clients as they realize their real estate goals.

Sherla understands the dynamics of the DMV real estate market and is an expert at educating her clients so they make the best decisions for their needs. As an agent with Own Real Estate, Sherla utilizes exclusive marketing tools to widely expose her client’s listings across the internet, showing each property to its best advantage and ensuring each home stands apart from its competition. Sherla believes that education is essential to success and has completed intensive training covering a variety of topics from marketing listings for quick sales to negotiating contracts and helping sellers in danger of foreclosure.

Sherla is a member of the Maryland REALTOR® (MR), Prince George’s County Association of REALTORS® (PGCAR) National Associations of REALTOR®(NAR) in addition to the Multiple Listing Service (MLS). Sherla feels that being a REALTOR® is about more than joining professional associations, she believes a Realtor should be involved in their communities as well. Sherla has served her community in many capacities from volunteering at her neighboring schools to performing home buyer’s workshops for educators.

Sherla understands the stresses and hassles involved in buying, selling, and the relocation process and strives to alleviate these concerns for her clients. In Sherla’s free time, she enjoys reading, traveling, fitness classes and spending time with her family.

Whether buying or selling, be it your first home, your last home, or building your investment portfolio, Sherla has the knowledge and experience to deliver the unsurpassed service you deserve. She can’t wait to help you achieve your real estate dreams.

Call, text, or email Sherla today to start making your real estate dreams a reality.
