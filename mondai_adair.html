About Me

Mondai has worked continually with some aspect of homes and building design since 1994 when she graduated from Tennessee with both architectural and interior design degrees. She has worked at top corporate design companies for 13 years, starting as an intern while in college. She began her 2nd career in real estate in 2004 and became "Rookie of the Year 2005" for best office production in her first year. She joined a business partnership with S4 managing partner Kevin Craig in 2004 and has worked with him since.

Mondai has maintained the Multi-Million Dollar Producer Award with the South East Valley Association of Realtors by loving what she does. She's passionate about educating her clients with making the best real estate decisions. She uses her background of project management and focuses on customer service to successfully help buyers, sellers and investors with all their real estate needs. Mondai strives to provide each client with the best real estate experience as possible throughout the process. Mondai specializes in traditional and luxury homes, new homes consultations, move-up home buyers, first time home-buyers, relocation purchases, investor purchases/fix & flips/VRBO rentals and short sales.

Mondai has lived in Tempe Arizona for the past 13 years. She's married and has a son who had graduated from ASU. She also has a very spoiled little 2 year old grand-baby and Westie named Max.
