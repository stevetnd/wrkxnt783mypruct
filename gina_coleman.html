About Me

Representing the greater Los Angeles area buyers and sellers, Gina's enthusiastic, caring nature and can-do-attitude is ideal for identifying each client's desires to skillfully guide them to attain their Real Estate goals. Raised in Mid-city Los Angeles, Gina appreciates and understands the many unique benefits of life in Los Angeles with its unmatched culture, beauty and amazing restaurants and entertainment!! Gina has knowledge in all aspects of the Real Estate process with her start in Television Production and then on to Real Estate Appraising proceeding her success in selling Real Estate. In her spare time she also enjoys family time, working out, traveling and spending time on a beach in Sunny Southern California!!!
