About Me

Growing up as a Navy brat gave me a childhood that had special memories of places we lived but there were also drawbacks not having the stability of a long time home. I watched my mom and stepdad struggle with money for years, they both worked really hard after he left the Navy and they just always seemed to never get ahead. Financing, budgeting, the true cost of housing, credit and the lack of access to information in those times just perpetuated a lifetime of wrong decisions. I've spent my lifetime making sure that didn't happen to me, my family, my friends or my clients over the years. In 1998 I opened a mortgage company and stayed in that industry for more than a decade, always holding my real estate license at the same time to invest for myself and friends. As a Realtor with a mortgage background I can serve my clients so much better navigating financing options available and properly valuing real estate. You can make wise informed decisions knowing my decades of finance and real estate experience is standing beside you. Let me help you make your real estate transaction the success you deserve. You're a hero and I want to show you my appreciation for your service to the community! The amount you receive back on your transaction is just a big hug from me saying "thank you" once again!
