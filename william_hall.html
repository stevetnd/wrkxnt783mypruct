About Me

Here at Racine Realty, we pride ourselves on truly a more personal touch - providing our clients with a level of service, honesty, integrity, and expertise that is unparalleled. Well after closing, we are committed to building lasting relationships with our clients. Will's passion for real estate led him to this path and he's poised to have his real estate valuation background (commercial and residential) and his charitable ways benefit you. Will is excited to be a preferred partner with Teacher Next Door and give back to our area’s fabulous teachers, non-instructional school staff/administration, medical field professionals, police officers, firefighters/EMTs, soldiers, vets, and government employees when they are looking to buy or sell a home, townhome, or condominium. Will covers Cook, DuPage, Kane, Kendall, Lake, McHenry & Will counties (surrounding communities by request). Will currently resides in DuPage County with his wife, Lori, their daughter, Samantha, and their rescue dog/mascot, Roscoe. You will hear from Lori as she helps with client service and coordinating appointments. Samantha currently attends IMSA, the Illinois Mathematics & Science Academy. In Will’s free time, his hobbies include gourmet cooking, sports, being active in the community, and watching his daughter swim competitively.
