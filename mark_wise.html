About Me

“Knowledge is knowing that tomato is a fruit. Wisdom is knowing not to put it in a fruit salad.” –T-Shirt Quote.

Mark has spent his life helping and educating others—teaching them the difference between knowledge and wisdom. Whether serving in ministry (10 years total in 2 churches), teaching undergraduate religion courses (5 years, Anderson University), managing a non-profit (3 years, Family Promise of Anderson County), or educating his clients in the best financing options, Mark was put on this earth to impart wisdom to others in order to help them make intelligent life decisions. As a Broker and a licensed Real Estate Instructor in the state of South Carolina with over 500 hours of classroom instruction to his credit, Mark brings a variety of experience to bear on client transactions.

Mark has experience in commercial real estate sales and leasing as well as residential sales, residential and commercial buyer representation, real estate investment, and real estate instruction. In 2015, Mark created Team Wise Choice, a real estate team committed to providing knowledge, support, and the wisdom clients need to have a successful real estate experience. Mark personally oversees and mentors his team members, teaching them the difference between real estate knowledge and real estate wisdom. One of the first clients the team represented built a self-storage portfolio of over $4 million dollars in just 2 years.

When Mark is not serving his clients, he enjoys boating on the numerous Upstate South Carolina lakes with his wife Susan and his children Kelsey and Jackson. He also enjoys eating sushi with Kelsey and Jackson (Susan—not so much a sushi eater), a good cup of espresso-infused coffee, a good home brew, and a loud 70’s muscle car. Incidentally, he enjoys ripping of colloquial wisdom from T-Shirts worn by the elderly in a Thai restaurant as well.
