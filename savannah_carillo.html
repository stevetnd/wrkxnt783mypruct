About Me

I started my real estate journey over a year ago when I found out I was having my first baby. My Dad, who is a Broker, finally convinced me after two years to try out this industry and see how I liked it. I was managing a bar at the time while trying to find a career in Anthropology with no luck. So I became his Transaction Coordinator and learned the business from a successor himself. I realized that this is what I am supposed to do. I wanted to find the right job that embraced me with people of my community while in the beautiful state of California and I did. I pursued my license and here we are.

I pride myself in transparency, integrity, and knowledge so that I can get my clients to their end goal as home owners. This is important to me because it creates a solid foundation to do great business together.

During my free time, my family and I embrace our time together. My husband serves in the U.S. Navy on submarines that consists of him going out to sea frequently. We love taking the family out to the dog beaches so our sweet pups, Beau and Wyatt, can play in the ocean. We love being outdoors and taking our one-year-old Joaquin out and about around town. We love walking around farmers markets, going to local shops and and trying out new restaurants. I also enjoy giving back to my community by joining the beach clean-ups with I Love A Clean San Diego. I gather with people of my community to clean up our city and contribute to making our planet a better place to live.
