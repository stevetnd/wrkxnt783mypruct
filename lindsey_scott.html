About Me

For 20 years I have owned and directed upscale fine art galleries from Tysons Corner, VA to Boca Raton and West Palm Beach. FL. I have built trusting and lasting relationships with clients over the years; many coming back often for guidance and expertise. In 2015, I got my real estate license and today spend my time marketing real estate and working with buyers and sellers in Palm Beach County and up to Port Saint Lucie.

My goal with buyers is to listen to their wants and needs. And sometimes help them figure out what is best for them; whether that be figuring out what the right location is or assessing what type of home fits their needs most. It is my job to assist in the best way that I can to make the process as smooth as possible.

When assisting sellers with marketing their property, I have created a proven strategy that makes their property standout against the competition and get the results they desire. This strategy includes 3D Property showcase, full motion video, professional photography, luxurious full color brochures, premium placement on Realtor.com, Zillow, Truilia and Homes.com. I also have many years of marketing and graphic design experience and attended the Art Institute for Web Design and Interactive Media. I have the skillset to make your property stand out and get the attention it needs to sell. And as the market adjusts, I keep up-to-date on market trends so that my clients are always ahead of the curve.

I've been a member of the Leadership Business Council and a West Palm Beach Arts & Entertainment District committee member.
