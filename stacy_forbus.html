About Me

Stacy is a lifelong resident of Monroe County and an insurance agent for fourteen years, Stacy offers well-rounded knowledge of home ownership to her clients. A member of Northeast Mississippi Board of Realtors, Mississippi Association of Realtors, National Association of Realtors, Stacy has a passion for real estate and helping clients achieve the best outcome in every transaction. She finished 2019, 2020, AND 2021 as Top 10 in sales for the NEMSBR and number 1 in Monroe County all 3 years.

Stacy is a graduate of Amory High School and earned her BA in Education from the University of Mississippi. She worked as an educator for twelve years and is an active member of First Baptist Church. Stacy has three grown children: Rob and his wife Caitlin of Tupelo, MS, Wesley and his wife Caroline and son Wells of Amory, MS, and Addie of Nashville, TN.
