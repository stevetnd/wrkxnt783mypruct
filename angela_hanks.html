About Me

Angela Hanks is a proud small-town native of Clarksville, VA, a gorgeous lake town bordering North Carolina. After spending 22 years in Richmond, Virginia, she relocated to North Carolina. Angela has dedicated the last 24 years of her life to people, process, and customer experience management in the Banking and Service Industry and brings immense customer focus and process management experience to bear when working with her real estate clients.

Angela Hanks attended VCU and Saint Paul's College and earned an Associate degree in Political Science and a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, with a concentration in Marketing. Her skills in client relationship management and project management give her a unique insight into how to negotiate buyers' wants/needs with sellers' goals and objectives.

Angela Hanks has successfully managed projects ranging in scope of 5 to 100 stakeholders and from 100k to 400M in revenues and has found that attention to detail, preparation, respect, trust and integrity are the key ingredients for success. These attributes coupled with her love of helping people realize their goals while enjoying the journey along the way makes her uniquely suited to serve real estate buyers and sellers in every housing market.

Angela has been blessed to own 4 homes in her lifetime and had the opportunity to serve as a homeowner co-builder for her family's forever home. She entered the Real Estate world in order to help her clients navigate through the excitement, decision-making and challenges that can arise in the home purchase/selling process and is ready to guide you through it all from prequalification, home search, and contract to closing on your new home. If you are interested in building your dream home, look no further...Angela's heart beats wildly for the home-build process.

Angela Hanks is married and lives in Oxford, NC with her husband and two teenagers. She spends her free time reading, writing, teaching abundant life principles, enjoying family and friends and binging on all things HGTV. As your Realtor® you can trust Angela to respect your time, your input and your investment when it comes to your real estate needs.
