About Me

The Realtor You Can Count On...With Every Move You Make! Denise is a dedicated and proficient Realtor that brings almost 2 decades of experience in negotiation and communication to her clients. She is an expert at connecting with others, identifying needs, and going the extra mile. Through her proven track record, she has built her reputation as a go-getter and problem-solver.

Her inspiration for a career in real estate comes from a deep desire to help her clients fall in love with where they live. The process of closing one chapter of your life to enter another can be daunting and complex. But with Denise, you’re in good hands. Whether it’s helping you sell your house at the right price, or helping you discover your ideal home, she goes above and beyond to provide you the support you need.

When you work with Denise, you get more than an agent. You get a world-class professional on your team that listens to you and understands your needs. She will help you navigate the emotions of buying or selling a home and guide you to the best outcome. She has the communication skills and the ability to recognize the challenges of all the parties involved with each transaction. She works well under pressure maintaining a clear perspective; her confidence in the transaction helps assure your navigation through any real estate transaction.

She prides herself in providing exceptional service for all her clients’ unique needs. Her commitment to helping home owners achieve their goals, and supporting them throughout the entire process has helped Denise excel in the real estate business. First time home buyer classes, short sales, foreclosures, second home buyers, relocation sellers, and investor income properties are just some of her areas of expertise. Whether you are looking to purchase or looking to sell your single family home, Denise is prepared to tackle all of your real estate needs.
