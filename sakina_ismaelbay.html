About Me

Sakina Ismaelbay grew up in Madagascar, an island nation well-known for lemurs, which lies just east of Africa’s Mozambique. Later in life, she moved to Paris, France for ten years where she earned a Master’s Degree in Human Resources. Through her life journeys, she has learned to speak six languages: French, Malagasy, Hindi, Gujrati, English, and German.

After meeting her future husband, Shoquat, she moved to the United States in 2012 to start a new journey in life. Sakina has now been happily married to her Husband for nearly ten years. Several years into their marriage, they were blessed with one of the greatest gifts, the gift of new life, their son, Adam.

Sakina started Real Estate in 2015. She closed 25 real estate transactions and earned the much-coveted recognition from her Broker as ‘Rookie of the Year’. The following year was a big year for her. She was again recognized by her Broker as the ‘Top Producer’ for Fathom Realty in the State of Texas and second in production across all markets in the country, while pregnant with her first son, Adam. She continues to rank in the top tier of producers in Fathom Realty each year after. In 2016, amidst labor pains, she did what every first-time mother would do: grab the suitcase, call the doctor and … write a real estate contract! Sakina and Sho have been blessed with the most perfect little boy and since Real Estate is a family business, you might meet her little assistant in training!

In a market saturated with Realtors®, Sakina quickly learned to standout amongst the crowd and adopted her business mantra of “More Service Than You’re Used To”. Sakina understands that decisions surrounding her clients Real Estate needs are likely some of the most important decisions of their life. Her constant education surrounding all matters Real Estate best equip her to serve her clients. She remains grateful for all the wonderful families that she serves. She understands the importance of having a client’s trust and treats each transaction as if were her own. Serving and protecting herclient’s best interests are her top priorities. Sakina conducts her business with honesty, transparency and integrity by putting her client’s needs first!

Sakina’s unique background and work ethic has set her for success in the Real Estate world. Her Master’s Degree in Human Resources helps her understand the psychology behind decision’s made in a transaction and makes her the ideal negotiator. Sakina’s attention to detail is often one of the most recognized traits when serving her clients. She is passionate about ensuring each detail of the transaction is properly addressed. Lastly, Sakina has built quality relationships with some of the top professionals in Real Estate and related industries, in an effort to provide “More Service Than You’re Used To”. She surrounds herself with the best because that is the level of service she expects for her clients. Regardless of her client’s needs, Sakina has the connections to provide them with the appropriate support for their given need.

Sakina is looking forward to serving you!
