About Me

Fabiana and Gabriela Rodriguez with Rodriguez Realty Partners are not only sisters but also seasoned real estate professionals with over two decades of experience. They were born in Uruguay, raised in New York City, and have been enjoying the South Florida lifestyle since 2002. This team is not only tenacious and candid but also service-driven, dedicated to providing the ultimate customer experience with integrity and professionalism. They have earned the respect of their community through their unmatched devotion to their clients and moxie personalities. Every day is an opportunity to exceed their customers' expectations and to continue to grow, individually and as a team. Their success is based almost exclusively on client referrals. They are humbled by the trust and loyalty that their clients have placed in them and are committed to providing the optimum level of service. To them, the greatest gift that the real estate profession has afforded has been the ability to help others through the use of their skills and diverse professional backgrounds. They firmly believe that every person involved in the transaction plays an important role towards a successful closing.
