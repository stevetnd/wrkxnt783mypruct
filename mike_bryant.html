About Me

We Exist to provide Excellence in every aspect of the real estate transaction, operating with Integrity and a Focus on Service.

Mike has been in the real estate industry since way back in 1984. Starting as a young “investor”, Mike purchased his first rental house – an 80-year old house in downtown Muncie, IN.

With that experience, Mike caught the “real estate bug”. Moving to Lee’s Summit from Indiana in 1996, Mike quickly got into the real estate market locally, purchasing a 4-plex in Raytown. Mike, along with a partner, also completed some “fix-and-flips” in the Hot market of the early 2000’s; and from 2011 through 2014, Mike acquired 15 properties, most of them single-family homes, to hold as rentals or sell as Lease/Purchase offerings, looking to assist the many buyers who had been effectively shut out of home ownership due to the “mortgage meltdown” of 2007-2008.

In 2014, Mike obtained his real estate license and quickly became a top producing agent. In 2016, Mike obtained his Broker license. Over his career he has completed nearly 200 transactions, working with many buyers, investors, and sellers in all price ranges. He serves all of the Kansas City Metro area.

In 2020, Mike co-founded The Bryant Ferguson Group with Michael Ferguson, joining eXp Realty. Mike and Michael have a shared vision of Excellence, Integrity, and Service.
