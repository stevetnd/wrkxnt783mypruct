About Me

I have lived in the greater Salt Lake Area for 39 years and have had the privilege of professionally service the south end of the valley Draper area for 13 years. After graduating high school and starting my own construction company I quickly learned the importance of hard work, dedication, loyalty, attention to detail and “saying what you do, do what you say, no surprises!” It was in 2003 at the age of 23 that I decided after meeting my soon to be wife that I wanted to connect more with others and the community I was working in, I became a Realtor.

While my time being a Realtor has certainly had its economic ups and downs one thing will always remain the same. I love helping people!! After countless transactions I can say that I have been blessed to have a clientele that I consider part of my family. While I love being a Realtor my other favorite profession is being a husband to my wife Stephanie who I consider to be my best friend. A father to my amazing, talented, charismatic and loving daughter Olivia. Last but not least a Son to two amazing parents that have always supported me and taught me the importance of being a man of your word.

I have been truly blessed in my life to enjoy a long career in a very exciting industry but also enjoy every opportunity to unwind and do some of the things I love to do. I love getting out to enjoy everything Utah has to offer. From hiking and mountain biking the many trails, to fly fishing some of the best waters around. At night I enjoy preparing meals for my family, sitting by the fire taking in the mountain views and connecting with my inner child and play video games. At the end of the day I enjoy nothing more than serving the community I live in and being a Realtor!
