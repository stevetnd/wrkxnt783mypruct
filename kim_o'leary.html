About Me

Hey there! I’m Kim, and I’d like to tell you a little about me…

I was born and raised in The Bronx, NY. After graduating from Hofstra University with a Bachelor’s degree in Communication, I worked in the TV/Radio & Music industry in New York City. When I started a family, we moved to the Putnam Valley, NY area where I began my Real Estate Career in 2001. I went on to become a Real Estate Broker in 2004.

In 2005, my children & I moved to sunny Peoria Arizona. My role in the real estate industry changed a little, and after processing Real Estate Loans for a few years, I decided to return to school, and obtained a Master’s Degree in Education from Arizona State University in 2008. Because I wanted to spend more time with my children while they were little, I took a short break from real estate I and began teaching in the Peoria Unified School District in 2008.

I returned to the Real Estate industry in 2013 (part time) and joined Arizona Premier Realty in Glendale, Arizona. In 2018, I left teaching, and became a Full Time Associate Broker! I mainly work in the North West Valley, in the areas of Peoria, Glendale, North/West Phoenix, Surprise and Sun City. Living & working in the West Valley for over 10 years means that I know the schools and the area, which helps me better serve my clients.

When not busy selling real estate, you can usually find me spending time with my family, taking in a ball game, attending a concert or visiting one of the many community/city festivals and activities going on in the West Valley. I love living in the West Valley and am eager to share my knowledge of the area with you. Whether you’re moving across the country, or across the street, I want to help you buy or sell your next home here too!
