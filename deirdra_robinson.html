About Me

Deirdra “Dee-Dee” Robinson is a Jacksonville native and a graduate of Andrew Jackson High School (1988). She attained a degree in Elementary Education from the University of North Florida (1991). As a Navy wife of 20 years, she has resided and taught school in various cities including Puerto Rico, Memphis, TN, Pensacola, FL and Virginia Beach, VA. While in Memphis, she completed a master’s degree in Instructional Design and Technology at the University of Memphis(1996).

Invariably, Dee-Dee found herself assisting military friends with relocating to various areas by conducting research on schools, jobs, housing market, etc., which prompted a lifetime passion for real estate. A person’s ideal job is what he or she finds him or herself doing for free or for pleasure. For Dee-Dee, that dream job is real estate; she absolutely loves working as a real estate professional, educating and assisting people with becoming homeowners. She proceeded to get a Virginia real estate license in 2002 before deciding to come home to Florida where she has been licensed in real estate since 2006.

While residing in nearby Clay County, Dee-Dee is an expert of the surrounding counties as well (Duval, St. Johns, Nassau). Dee-Dee was fortunate to open DD Home Realty Inc, a full-service real estate company, in 2016. She is a Military Relocation Professional (MRP) as well as a Certified Residential Specialist (CRS). Her experience includes working with veterans, first-time home buyers, as well as investors with the purchase of short sales, foreclosures, new construction and resale homes. Dee-Dee is also skilled as a listing agent, listing, marketing, and helping sellers to get their homes sold in a timely manner.
