About Me

I am a licensed Realtor and an Arizona native. I was born in Tucson and have lived all over the Phoenix area for the last 38 years. I know the Valley well! I have experience with everything from entry level homes to million dollar estates. I am in a group of top agents who support each other. No deal is too challenging for me!

I take pride in helping buyers find the perfect home for them. I like to meet with people in person when possible before we go look at homes; I like to get to know my buyers and what their exact needs are so I can best support them. They then have an opportunity to ask me questions, so they are prepared and feel comfortable with me and the process as well. I educate, inform and advise my home buyers throughout the entire process. I also enjoy working with home sellers, providing them with the many options I have available that will best fit their lives. I can accommodate people who want a quick sell, or do not want to deal with showings, and of course the traditional home sale with showings and/or open houses. I have a list of vendors available to my clients. My buyers and sellers appreciate how quickly I respond to them. I strive to build lifelong relationships; if you have a real estate question at any time I want to be your friendly expert.

Outside of real estate I love spending time with my family and friends. I am married and together we have five great boys and an awesome daughter-in-law. I also have three fur babies. I'm a scrapbooker and enjoy outdoor activities.

I look forward to getting to know you and helping you with your real estate goals and dreams!
